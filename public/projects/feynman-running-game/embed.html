Feynman奔跑游戏 - iframe嵌入代码

您可以使用以下代码将Feynman奔跑游戏嵌入到您的网站中：

固定尺寸 (推荐尺寸: 1920x1080)

<iframe 
  src="https://html.feynmanxie.cc/projects/feynman-running-game/index.html" 
  width="1920" 
  height="1080" 
  frameborder="0" 
  allowfullscreen
  allow="accelerometer; camera; microphone; gamepad"
></iframe>

响应式设计

<div style="position: relative; padding-bottom: 56.25%; height: 0; overflow: hidden;">
  <iframe 
    src="https://html.feynmanxie.cc/projects/feynman-running-game/index.html" 
    style="position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%;" 
    frameborder="0" 
    allowfullscreen
    allow="accelerometer; camera; microphone; gamepad"
  ></iframe>
</div>
